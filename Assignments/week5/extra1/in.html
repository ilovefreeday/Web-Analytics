        VLife NYC is my go to place for lunch. I have probably come here everyday for the past month and a half! The staff is super friendly and will go out of their way to help you out. It's quick, reasonably priced and delicious. My favorite is the new eggplant wrap and the hummus wrap. If you don't want to wait, call ahead and the food will be ready upon arrival. The credit card minimum is $10 though, so make sure you have cash otherwise I usually get the kombucha drink- just don't shake it or it explodes. I highly recommend this place to vegans and non vegans alike, you will definitely not be sorry!

        V Life is a must try. I was going there to get one of my favorite sandwiches from Loving Hut to discover that it was now a new place called V Life. I was initially impressed with the upgrade of bright green color on their walls. They also have better high chair and larger table seating than Loving Hut had. I got the philly cheese steak to go for $9 bucks. It was definitely worth it and they had a variety of different sauces to customize it with. Today I went back and decided I would stay. There were only two other people in there. In between my order and theirs, something happened with their stove/cooking system and they couldn't prepare my order. I was given my money back with an apology. Although looking quite disappointed, I totally understood. I overheard the cashier say they are currently in an experimental stage with the food to see what the public likes and by July they should be officially ready. Their menu definitely has a diverse selection of good vegan food including both seitan and tofu. I will definetly be coming back all summer!

        Sometimes you want to eat some good vegan grub and maybe still feel like you are indulging a bit: V Life satisfies on both counts! I played it safe my first visit with a Go Green smoothie and some fried tofu and guys, it was awesome! The smoothie had a smooth consistency (nothing irks me more than a poorly mixed smoothie) with just the right ratio of greens to fruit. And the tofu was spot on. They flash fry it here so it cuts down on the oil, too. It may not be as crispy to others likings but for me, it was perfecto. Can't wait to try my next meal!
